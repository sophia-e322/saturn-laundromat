Welcome!

This is a website for all my coding projects, art pieces, and whatever else I'm proud of. Please have a look around and enjoy your stay! -Sophia (she/her)

Why the name Saturn's Laundromat?

I wanted something that sounded dreamy and pensive but still untimatly mundane. Plus, I read a story from Cricket magazine awhile ago that talked about all the random things you lost ending up in Saturn's rings, and I always thought that idea sounded really cute.

How'd you get into html coding?

I saw a post online talking about neocities, and I went to go look around at all the cool websites different people had on there. It really inspired me so I decided to start my own website, and after lots of googling and experiments I made this!

What book are you currently reading?

I'm reading Children of Time by Adrian Tchaikovsky, a sci-fi book that explores themes of evolution, time, and life. I would recommend it to anyone who wants to read about the evolution of a spider society!
